About Anamnet Construction

Building Trust Through Experience, Excellence, and Sustainability

Our Company

Anamnet Construction is a leading Nigerian enterprise specializing in lead and zinc mining operations and premium property construction services. With over a decade of experience, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner for demanding projects across the continent.

Our success is built on a foundation of technical expertise, operational excellence, and an unwavering commitment to safety and environmental stewardship. We combine modern mining techniques with sustainable practices to minimize ecological impact while maximizing resource efficiency.

In the construction sector, we deliver residential, commercial, and infrastructure projects that exceed client expectations. Our team of skilled professionals brings meticulous attention to detail, innovative design solutions, and transparent project management to every undertaking.
